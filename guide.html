How to Use Smart Bus

Complete guide for student commuters

🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to the Smart Bus Management System! To track your bus, simply enter your Roll Number on the login page.

Ensure you are using the roll number provided during registration (e.g., ES24AD131).

🗺️ Live Tracking Features

Campus Map

See your bus moving in real-time along the optimized route path.

Smart ETA

Get precise arrival times based on traffic and live speed data.

Stop Pins

Click any stop on the map to pin it as your destination.

❔ Frequently Asked Questions

What if my bus isn't moving?
Check the "System Status" widget on the login page. If it says "Normal", allow a few seconds for data refresh. If the bus is in a stop, it will show as "In Stop".

How do I contact the driver?
Once logged in, your driver's contact details are available in the dashboard sidebar. Use the "Call Driver" button for emergencies only.
